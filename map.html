MAP

Although legally defined as a country, Fralk has no centralized government. Instead, Fralk is recognized and protected by surrounding countries for its longstanding history and ultimately left to develop naturally. This means that any mapping of Fralk is done through observation and documentation of independent, traveling cartographers. Maps of Fralk vary widely in accuracy and none can be considered exhaustive. This map was drawn by Aikesh Stod, an adventurer from the neighboring country of Ulledon who spent the last several decades of their life exploring and serving the communities of Fralk
