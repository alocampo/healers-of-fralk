SUN

As the weather changes and the sun grows scarce, plants that require large amounts of sunlight cycle out. However, with an item such as this handheld sun, it is possible to extend the longevity of such plants throughout harsher seasons. While a sun is a particularly rare item, these kinds of heating and lighting solutions are often utilized by plantfolk who would otherwise have to hibernate or migrate to a warmer country during colder periods.

Notes

Despite the capability of immense heat, the sun did not seem to have any effect on the snow or environment surrounding the backpack. Light effects are easily contained and heat effects seem to be triggered by physical contact with organic matter.
